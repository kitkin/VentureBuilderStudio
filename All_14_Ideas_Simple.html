7. AI Medicine Cabinet

Scan your medicines. AI lists them. Tracks expiry. Tells you what to take for symptoms. Warns about bad mix. Reminds to restock.

Problem

Home has 15–30 medicines. People forget what is for what. Many are expired (30%+). They don’t know about bad combinations. At night with sick kid — 30 min search.

What we do

  • Scan barcode or photo. AI adds to list. You set expiry.
  • Push when expiry soon. You type symptoms → AI says what to take from your list.
  • Checks if medicines clash. Reminds to buy more. Family can share one cabinet.

Price

Free up to 20 medicines. Then $4.99 per month

Like

Medisafe (only reminders), MedBay (only expiry)

Who uses it

Parent 30–45. 20+ medicines at home. Half expired. Kid sick at night — what to give?

Risk

Small market. This type of customer is not an early adopter.
